About Us

We are seasoned Petroleum & Lubricants Wholesalers, catering to the needs of small to medium enterprises in distribution, trucking, mining, and retail. With over 30 years of combined experience in the petroleum industry, our expertise goes beyond wholesale, encompassing property developments and depots. Supported by associate companies in management and finance, we offer an equitable empowerment solution and a robust business model. Our long-term goal is to develop Storage Facilities, wholesale depots, and on-site gas & diesel installations, providing turnkey solutions across industries.

Fuelling is Our Game:

Established in 2016 as an independent BBBEE LEVEL 2 petroleum distributor, we officially started full trade in 2020. Committed, responsive, and reliable leadership takes pride in considering you as our valued customer. We are flexible to meet specific requirements, offering competitive prices. Committed to delivering the highest quality petroleum products, we source from reputable oil majors, ensuring product security. Our current services include storage tank provision, bulk fuel transportation, and supply of various products. Future plans involve expanding our offerings to include Diesel 10 ppm, Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG), Biofuels, Bitumen, Asphalt, and other Black Products.

Management Credentials

Our team, comprising seasoned advisors and managers with extensive careers in the Petroleum Industry, leverages their deep experience for our business success. Key team members bring valuable fuel trading expertise, crucial for our business model. We maintain robust partnerships with local Oil Marketing Companies and continuously research to enhance our services and stay current in the energy sector. Central to our mission is ensuring we have the right people and resources to deliver excellence.

We offer customised payment solutions with appropriate risk management and are committed to being responsive, available almost 24/7 to address queries promptly. Our young, skilled team excels in automation, driving solutions-focused processes. We enjoy strong support from suppliers, mentors, and financial institutions, and prioritise building solid relationships with clients and suppliers. Upholding trust and integrity with stakeholders and adhering to industry standards, we ensure sourcing only the best quality products.
